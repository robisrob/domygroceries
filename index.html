Do my groceries

Do my groceries will be the first application of the helpingoldies project.

Helping Oldies

The helpingoldies project aims to provide tools to help older people that are not so mobile.

Do my groceries

This app will provide a tool to older people where they can make groceries list and ask other people to do their groceries for them.

Help

This project is still in its early days and is desperately searching other people that want to help.
